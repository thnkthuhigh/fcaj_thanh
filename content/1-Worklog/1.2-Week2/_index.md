### Week 2 Objectives:

* Understand the role of EC2, S3, and IAM in deploying a backend system on AWS.
* Practice creating servers, storage buckets, and basic access-control configurations.
* Build a foundation for later infrastructure and application-deployment work.

### Week 2 Achievements:

* Understood the role of EC2, S3, and IAM in a backend system deployed on AWS.
* Learned how to create servers, storage buckets, and basic access-control settings.
* Built a stronger foundation for later application deployment and infrastructure work.
